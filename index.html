About Us

What is zero waste?



“Zero Waste is a set of principles focused on waste prevention that encourages the redesign of resource life cycles so that all products are reused. The goal is for no trash to be sent to landfills, incinerators, or the ocean.” -Wikipedia

Who Are We?



Zero Waste Michigan is an inclusive, student-led organization focusing on reducing waste on campus and in our own lives. Our goal is to create a community of people interested in the low-impact lifestyle and to spread information about the environmental effects of waste. Our purpose is to be a voice against unnecessary waste on campus and to create a community for those interested in living with less waste.

What do we do?



We look for ways we can reduce our footprint at the individual as well as the university level, whether that be through popup thrift shops, campus food waste initiatives, or making DIY cleaning products. Education is important to us, and we love to her from guest lectures and members’ knowledge so that we can educate others.
